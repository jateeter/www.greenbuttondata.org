Green Button is an industry-led effort that responds to a White House call-to-action: provide electricity customers with easy access to their energy usage data in a consumer-friendly and computer-friendly format via a "Green Button" on electric utilities' website. Green Button is based on a common technical standard adopted voluntarily by utilities that allows software developers and other entrepreneurs to create applications and tools to help consumers make the most of their energy usage information.

By putting customers in control of their own energy data, they can choose which tools and services can help them manage their energy performance.

Companies are already developing applications using Green Button data to help people choose the best rate plan for their use patterns; deliver customized energy-efficiency tips; offer easy-to-use tools to size and finance rooftop solar panels; and conduct virtual energy audits that can cut costs for building owners and speed the initiation of retrofits.

Creating new ways to help consumers understand and manage their energy use and the environmental impact of that usage is a field ripe for American innovation.
